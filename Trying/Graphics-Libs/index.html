Graphic Libs Test

Two.js

Draw lines with two.js

Pixi.js

Draw lines and line mouseover detect with pixi.js

web gl render with canvas fallback

Easy to use, like the fact to draw objects and individual setting of hitarea


plexx.js

Not used yet, waiting for last bug fixes


flotr2.js

Time Series with flotr2.js

Nice Canvas Lib, easy to use and expand. Favorite for building the Prototype



dygraph.js

Time Series with Dygraph.js

Also a nice Canvas Lib, has nice modules. Zoom for example. Is my second favorite after flotr2.js



jqplot.js

Time Series with jqplot.js

Many functionality, i like the zoom and axes function. Could also be a good lib for the Prototype



epoch.js

Time Series with epoch.js

In some kind a nice Canvas Lib, has low functionality and the axes ticks also could be better.



nvd3.js

Time Series with nvd3.js

SVG Lib with good functionality. Individual modules could be better seperated. Easy to use.



rickshaw.js,chartkick.js,flotcharts and many more

tried...but did not convinced me of the technology and usage
